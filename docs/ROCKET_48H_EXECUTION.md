# ROCKET 48H EXECUTION (Zero Budget)

## Mission
Get first 2 leads in 48 hours using only free channels and current stack:
- Website form -> Telegram one-tap
- Facebook Page + local groups
- Nextdoor Business posts
- Google Business Profile posts/photos

## Agent pipeline (mandatory every task)
1. Perplexity: source/policy/fact collection
2. Gemini: Google/policy second pass
3. ChatGPT: final strategy + copy + release decision
4. DeepSeek: bulk variants + consistency + packaging

## 48-hour plan

### Hour 0-2 (Setup burst)
1. Publish 1 Facebook before/after post (same project visual only).
2. Publish 1 Nextdoor business post (same offer, platform-adapted text).
3. Publish 1 GBP update post + 3 new photos.
4. Confirm Telegram one-tap panel works from a new test lead.

Success metric:
- 3 live posts + 1 successful test lead in Telegram.

### Hour 2-8 (Distribution burst)
1. Post 1 more Facebook post with different hook and same CTA.
2. Share into 5 relevant local groups (non-duplicate first paragraph).
3. Publish 1 Nextdoor neighborhood post with specific service use case.
4. Update GBP Q&A (3 practical questions + answers).

Success metric:
- 10+ direct clicks to site/WhatsApp/call paths.

### Hour 8-24 (Conversion burst)
1. Answer every inbound message in less than 15 minutes.
2. Use one-tap templates only (taken, askaddr, askphoto, cb15).
3. Request 2-6 photos on every qualified lead.
4. Send one follow-up at +3h if no response.

Success metric:
- 1st qualified lead by end of Day 1.

### Hour 24-48 (Scale burst)
1. Publish 2 additional posts (FB + Nextdoor) with a new service angle.
2. Publish 1 GBP update with urgency slot wording.
3. Repost best performing creative with updated first line.
4. Continue SLA < 15 min and close to call/site visit.

Success metric:
- 2 total leads by hour 48.

## Hard gates (no exceptions)

2. No before/after creative with mixed scenes.
3. No high-risk claim without official source.
