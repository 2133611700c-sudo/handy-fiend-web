## 2. GOOGLE LOCAL SERVICES ADS (LSA) - FREE UNTIL YOU EARN

### What is LSA?
- Google's "request a quote" button for local services
- Shows above organic results
- **You only pay when someone requests a quote** (~$5-15 per lead)
- Better conversion than regular Ads (pre-qualified)

### Requirements
See `/docs/PHASE-B-LEGAL-GUARDRAIL.md` for detailed checklist.

**TL;DR:**
- [ ] CSLB decision (Option A or B)
- [ ] GBP 100% complete (above)
- [ ] General Liability Insurance ($50-200/mo)
- [ ] Business license

### Setup (After above done)
```
1. Go to https://ads.google.com/local
2. Click "Create account"
3. Verify insurance certificate
4. Set service areas (LA county ZIPs)
5. Set quote price (if hourly: "$150 service call")
6. Wait for approval (5-10 days)
7. Customers click "Request Quote" ‚Üí they call you
8. You get charged $5-15 per verified lead
```

### Cost Breakdown
- **Service call leads:** $5-10 per lead (if customer books)
- **Paint/flooring leads:** $10-20 per lead
- **Expected conversion:** 20-30% of leads ‚Üí jobs
- **Example:** Get 10 leads/month = $80 cost, 3 convert = $50 CAC (cost per acquisition)
