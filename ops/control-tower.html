Daily 60-Minute Leadgen Runbook (No Ad Spend)

  1. Check inbound leads in Telegram and reply via one-tap panel within 3 minutes.
  2. Publish one before/after case on Facebook page with direct CTA to site form.
  3. Post one local service offer in compliant community thread (no unsolicited DM spam).
  4. Answer 10 local intent comments with short qualification script (ZIP + service + timing).
  5. Push one SEO update on site copy (city + service keyword on target section).
  6. Log lead source quality in a sheet (platform, response time, booked yes/no).
KPI targets: response SLA under 3 min, 10 qualified conversations/day, 2 bookings/day baseline.
